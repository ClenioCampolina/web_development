➢ Paragraphs, Italicize, Emphacize, Bold and Strong

There are two ways of Italicizing your text. If you choos ethe tag "i", it will only change the format of your text.
But if you use the tag "em", you also bring the message tat this text should be enphasized (it is an important part of the text.
This extra information is passed to the browser (and probably to search engines) and might even get different formating
depending on the condition. As shown below, visually in this case, it is not possible to notice any difference.
